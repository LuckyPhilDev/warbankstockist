## v1.3.0

### Added
- Characters can now be ignored on the assignment panel — they appear greyed out at the bottom and are skipped during automatic stock management.
- All warband characters are now visible on the assignment panel, not just those seen on the current character.

### Improved
- Profile editing is no longer tied to your current character — you can edit any profile from any character.
- Depositing and withdrawing via slash commands is more reliable.
- The profile panel now opens by default when you open the UI.
- Refreshed visual design to match the Midnight art style.
- The settings window no longer opens automatically when you log in.

### Fixed
- Fixed an issue where depositing could fail silently.
- Fixed a crash caused by a missing row text entry.
